"UrbanPulse: Navigating Bangkok's Dynamic Atmosphere" seeks to capture,contrast and compare the metropolis of Bangkok and notably its bustling and  traffic. Analyzing and overlapping the data in which is conducted under the circumstances in which it captures  the three main aspects of pollution in different ways; be it noise,air pollution or the distraction that comes with being stuck in hours-long traffic. In a sense, creating a disturbance atmospherically implementing various mediums in both static and dynamic.
